Poles and Zeroes provide us the values of frequencies for which given transfer function's denominator and numerator becomes zero. The analysis of poles and zeroes help's us to identify that wether the given system is stable or not. Whereas gain is the ratio of output obtained to the input given to the system. The main Aim of this lab is to help you to understand how can you use MATLAB code to obtain poles, zeroes and gain values of given transfer function.
